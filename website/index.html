ARTEMIS

Welcome to the Artemis website! Artemis is the robotics club sponsored by Abbotsleigh. We aim to make STEAM more accessible and prevalent to the girls at our school, and extend this further to the community, both in school and in the public.

You could say that we are just an all-girls team. You could say that we’re just a robotics club. But ARTEMIS is so much more than that. For us, ARTEMIS is a family, a place to belong. Regardless if people say STEM is “nerdy” or if people say they are not carriers of STEM, ARTEMIS stands to make a change. And it is thanks to FIRST that we can use STEM for a fairer world.
