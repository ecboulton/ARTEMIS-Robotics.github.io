Current Projects

FIRST Robotics Competition

Our robot for FRC is named Big Chungus, and we're building him as our main focus, so we can use him for upcoming competitons. He is designed for the "Destination: Deep Space" challenge, presented by Boeing.

Fawkes

Our old friend Fawkes was disassembled a while ago, but we've decided to revive him for upcoming events such as the Glow Festival and for future use in miscellaneous endeavours.

Recreating Vines

As a bit of fun, we've decided to use our off-season time to put our skills to practice in recreating videos from the late popular social media platform, "Vine". The seven-second videos it was home to were often made to be comedic and silly, and have given internet users all over the world a lot of enjoyment. In honour of its memory, since it shut down in 2016, we are putting a new spin on the old format.
